What is the Pacebi Alliance?
The Pacebi Alliance (often just shortend to Pacebi) is an open GRSM safe space for everyone. We exclude exclusionists.

Where is the name from?
"Pacebi" is a blend of "pan", "ace", and "bi". It is pronounced /pˈɛɪsbˌaɪ/ PAYS-bye.

I hear you all want to invade Denmark. What is this about?
Pacebi was created after a joke between several GRSM communities wanting to invade the Nordic countries. Don't worry, we aren't actually invading anywhere yet.

GRSM?
GRSM is an initialism for Gender, Romantic, and Sexual orientation Minority. We use it over LGBTQIA+ as it is more concise and inclusive.

I have an issue. How can I contact you?
In-server issues can go to tickets. For general questions, go to the Tumblr page. For serious ones, email the owner at daysant@proton.me.
